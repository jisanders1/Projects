Provider -health care professional who provides services to ChocoAn members

Manager - accesses the summary report

ChocoAn Member - customer who pays for the ChocoAn services

Operator - add new members to ChocAn, to delete members who have resigned, and
to update member records through ChocoAn Data Center

Provider directory- alphabetically ordered list of service names and corresponding service codes and fees; sent to the provider as an email attachment

Weekend report - a list of services provided to the member receiving this report; report also includes member's personal information in addition to the service descriptions

Billing report - the list of services he or she provided to ChocAn members; requires provider's verification to access

EFT - electronic funds transfer; provider's bank account are accredited with EFTs

Summary report - list of every provider to be paid that week, the number of consultations each had, and his or her total fee for that week; only given to the manager

ChocoAn Data Center - data center whose main job is to process billing information

Terminal - a device similar to a credit card service at a store; when used by providers, provider needs to enter their provider number
